How it works

Five steady steps to keep scope, due diligence, and decisions aligned to your objective.

1. Define the goal

Buy, sell, or stay—your plan sets the standard for every decision.

2. Scope the work

Identify what actually moves value, safety, or marketability; skip the noise.

3. Verify scope + options

Repair/replace/defer scenarios with assumptions documented for apples-to-apples contractor bids.

4. Prepare bid package

Scope notes, priorities, and timing considerations organized for licensed contractors you choose.

5. Review bids + decide

If work is needed, we help you get independent bids from licensed contractors you choose, then compare scope and fit.

Why real-estate-led scope planning beats random quotes

Most quotes answer “what will it cost?” before clarifying “what should be done first?” We start with your real estate objective, verify scope assumptions, and organize due diligence so bids are comparable and decisions are clear.

Right-sized improvements we help you scope

Planning guidance for light improvements that support transaction readiness and value priorities.

  • Interior paint, drywall repair, and trim/door tuning
  • Flooring repairs, transitions, and selective replacement
  • Lighting and electrical fixture updates with code awareness
  • Hardware swaps for doors, cabinets, and baths
  • Minor bath refresh: caulk, grout, fixtures, mirrors
  • Kitchen touch-ups: countersplash, pulls, and appliance fit
  • Exterior repairs: fascia/soffit, rails, steps, and siding patches
  • Weather and moisture mitigation: gutters, grading, seals
  • Safety/code items that can delay appraisals
  • Small carpentry closeout items
  • Photo/staging prep and clean jobsite standards
  • Trade sequencing aligned to your move timeline

Builder FAQs

Do I need to do work before selling?

Not always. We prioritize scope that supports your pricing and marketability goals, then outline options for contractor bidding.

How do you decide what’s worth doing?

We map scope to your goal, then price options with ranges so you can choose the smartest path.

Do you manage contractor work?

No. We provide planning and bid-review support; licensed contractors you choose perform the work and remain responsible for the work and warranties.

Do you provide contractor estimates?

No contractor estimates. We document scope assumptions and help compare independent contractor bids (licensed contractors you choose).

How do timelines work?

We set a calendar with lead times and sequencing; you get steady updates and a clear finish.

Do you replace full systems?

We do not perform construction contracting. We support due diligence and referrals so you can hire the right licensed specialist.
