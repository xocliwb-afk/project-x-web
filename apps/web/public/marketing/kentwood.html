Living in Kentwood, MI

Kentwood is an incorporated city in south-central Kent County covering about 20.9 square miles. Its boundaries and service areas vary across corridors shaped by I-96, M-6, 28th Street (M-11), Division Avenue, and East Paris Avenue, with additional complexity near Gerald R. Ford International Airport. Housing types and oversight can differ by era and by whether a property is within an HOA or condominium project; school district boundaries can also vary within “Kentwood” addresses and should be verified by official maps.

Living in Kentwood: homes, lifestyle, and local insight

Kentwood is a bustling, diverse community located directly south of Grand Rapids and adjacent to the Gerald R. Ford International Airport. It is one of the most accessible areas in the region, hosting a wide variety of businesses, international cuisine, and shopping centers. Its central location means that no matter where you work in West Michigan—be it downtown or the manufacturing hubs—your commute is likely short and simple.

The city takes pride in its extensive parks and recreation department, with favorites like Veterans Memorial Park providing ample space for sports and community gatherings. Kentwood is unique in that it is served by multiple school districts, including Kentwood Public Schools and Forest Hills, giving parents various educational choices. It is a melting pot of cultures and lifestyles, making it a vibrant place to live.

Real estate in Kentwood is known for being approachable and varied. You can find everything from budget-friendly bungalows and townhomes to larger executive homes in quiet subdivisions. If you are searching for homes for sale in Kentwood, you will find a competitive market that offers great value for square footage. Self-Directed Research Tools

Official resources to verify parcels, zoning, permits, schools, flood and wetlands, and transit.

  • City Maps & Plans (Kentwood) — Official city maps including zoning and planning resources.
  • Parcel & Boundary Lookup (Kent County GIS) — Parcel boundaries, ownership fields, and overlays for verification.
  • Permits & Records (BS&A Online) — Permit history, rental registration status, and municipal record lookup.
  • Flood Zone Verification (FEMA) — Official flood hazard lookup by address.
  • Wetlands Inventory (EGLE) — State-regulated wetlands inventory and related map layers.
  • School Assignment (Kentwood Public Schools) — District map and assignment information (verify by address).
  • District Boundary Verification (Kent ISD) — County-wide district boundary reference (Kentwood vs nearby districts).
  • Transit Routes & Maps (The Rapid) — Fixed routes, BRT stations, and schedules.
