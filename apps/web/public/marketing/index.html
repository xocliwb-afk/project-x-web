One Partner. Real Estate First. Twice the Expertise.

At Brandon Wilcox Home Group, we lead with strategy and back it with craftsmanship. Our integrated REALTOR®–Builder model eliminates guesswork—delivering expert real estate guidance with construction insight only when it drives real value.

⟡

Buy Smarter

We help you look beyond the staging and into the structure. With both a REALTOR®’s eye and a builder’s knowledge, we assess each home's condition and renovation potential before you make an offer. You'll get estimated improvement costs, reduce surprises, and move forward with a documented plan—whether you're buying in Grand Rapids, Ada, or anywhere in West Michigan.

✦

Sell for More

You don’t need to renovate everything—just the right things. We advise on targeted, high-impact upgrades that support your pricing strategy and timeline. Then we handle the listing, pricing, and marketing approach to position your home competitively and attract serious buyers. It’s disciplined, efficient, and built to reduce surprises.

⬚

Build with Confidence

Whether you're fixing up to sell or improving your new purchase, we coordinate only what moves the needle. From handyman work to light renovations, we scope projects to your goals—not a contractor’s upsell. With reliable trades, clear budgets, and steady oversight, we help you keep timeline and costs on track.
