What Clients Get

For Sellers: A focused plan that combines pricing and positioning strategy with practical pre-list scope to help reduce avoidable surprises.

For Buyers: Condition-focused guidance at showings, repair and improvement ranges, and negotiation support grounded in real-world cost and value. During showings, we point out visible condition items and common big-ticket risk areas when accessible, then map what to verify next.

Builder license informs scope/risk conversations only. Brokerage services do not include home inspection, engineering, or construction contracting.

Showing observations are not a home inspection, engineering opinion, or contractor estimate. Confirm condition and costs with licensed inspectors/engineers and written bids.

If work is needed, we help you get independent bids from licensed contractors you choose.
