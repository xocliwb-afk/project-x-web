REALTOR® + Builder

About Brandon Wilcox Home Group

One accountable partner in Grand Rapids and across West Michigan.

The Brandon Wilcox Home Group is led by Brandon Wilcox, a licensed REALTOR® and a licensed builder. We operate on a simple idea: clear scope and documented options. You get straight assessments, real numbers, and a plan aligned to your goal—buying smart, selling with a defined strategy, or planning next steps after close.

Brandon brings builder-license perspective to scope, sequencing, and risk conversations while staying real-estate-first. Known for direct answers and practical plans, he focuses on budget clarity, risk awareness, and steady communication across Grand Rapids and greater West Michigan.

Scope planning is optional and tailored to your goals. We recommend right-sized work only when it supports your priorities and risk tolerance. If the smartest move is to list as-is or buy move-in ready, we’ll say so.

What we do

✦

Licensed Realtor® — Buyer & Seller Representation

The Brandon Wilcox Home Group delivers real estate strategy with real-world insight. As a licensed REALTOR® with builder-license perspective, we help buyers and sellers prioritize decisions around pricing, condition, and negotiation. Whether you’re buying your first home or selling one you’ve outgrown, you get straight answers, smart strategy, and steady communication so you can move forward with clarity.

⬚

Scope Planning for Listing Prep — Advisory Support

Every transaction depends on details. We help you prioritize punch-list items, sequence the work, and compare contractor bids so prep decisions stay aligned with your listing strategy and timeline. Work is performed by licensed contractors under their own agreements.

⟡

Bid Review & Verification Planning

When a property needs updates, we document assumptions, triage risk, and map a verification plan before deadlines. Not a substitute for licensed inspections/engineering evaluations or contractor bids. Brokerage services are separate from any construction work.
