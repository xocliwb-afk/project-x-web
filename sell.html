A Clear Path to a Top-Dollar Sale

1. The Home Assessment

Dual-perspective walkthrough: a REALTOR® market analysis plus a builder condition assessment. Honest valuation as-is vs. with improvements.

2. Right-Sized Improvements

Based on goals, timeline, and budget, we recommend targeted, high-impact updates (paint, lighting, fixtures, curb appeal, safety/code). Optional and data-driven.

3. Strategic Listing & Marketing

Professional photography and video, sharp property copy, floor plans when relevant, and targeted exposure to qualified West Michigan buyers.

4. Negotiation, Inspection & Closing

Builder-level insight when requests arise. We price options accurately, negotiate credits vs. repairs, and keep appraisal and closing on track.

Seller FAQs

Do I have to renovate to list with the Brandon Wilcox Home Group?

No. We suggest work only when it improves net value or reduces risk. Many homes list as-is and sell for strong numbers.

How do you decide which updates are worth doing?

Market comps plus condition-based adjustments and cost-benefit modeling for West Michigan buyers and appraisers.

Can you manage all vendors?

Yes. We coordinate licensed and insured trades and maintain one schedule, one budget, and one standard of quality.

What if the buyer requests repairs after inspection?

We price options quickly, negotiate credits vs. repairs, and prioritize the path that preserves your net and timeline.
