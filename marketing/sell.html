Why sellers hire a REALTOR® + builder

Strong list prices stick when the home backs them up. We evaluate structure, systems, and finish quality before launch so the story matches the condition. That means fewer surprises during inspections and clearer appraisals. With one licensed REALTOR® and builder at the table, you get smart pricing, clean scopes, and vetted trades only when they move the needle. You stay in control of timeline and budget while presenting a polished home that stands up to due diligence across Grand Rapids and West Michigan.

Prep priorities that usually pay off

Practical, high-signal updates that clear objections and support value.

  • Curb appeal refresh: entry paint, lighting, and landscaping touch-ups
  • Safety/code items: GFCI, rails, detectors, and obvious corrections
  • Moisture and grading fixes to keep inspections clean
  • Lighting, hardware, and fixture swaps for a cohesive look
  • Tile, grout, and caulk refreshes in kitchens and baths
  • Minor carpentry and drywall repairs that remove buyer doubt
  • Flooring repairs or transitions where they matter most
  • HVAC/serviceables tuned and documented when beneficial
  • Photo-ready edits: declutter plans, staging light, and punch lists
  • Scope and timing built around your move, not a contractor’s upsell

Seller FAQs

Do I have to renovate before listing?

No. We recommend only targeted work that protects value, reduces risk, or speeds your sale.

How long does prep usually take?

Most plans run 7–21 days depending on scope and trade availability; we set a clear schedule up front.

How do you set list price?

We pair comps with condition-adjusted ranges and buyer tolerance so pricing is documented, not inflated.

Can you handle vendors and scheduling?

Yes. We don’t perform renovation work. We can help you prioritize prep and introduce reputable local pros. If you want to DIY, we’ll tell you where effort actually matters.
